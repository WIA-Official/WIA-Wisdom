# WIA-Wisdom

> **弘益人間 (홍익인간)** · Benefit All Humanity
> 9 Pillars of Human Wisdom for the 21st Century and Beyond

---

## Vision | 비전

**English:**
WIA-Wisdom is a comprehensive, open framework that systematically organizes humanity's accumulated wisdom across all traditions, philosophies, sciences, and cultures. Like the historic Tripitaka Koreana (팔만대장경) which preserved Buddhist wisdom for a millennium, WIA-Wisdom aims to create a living, evolving repository of human knowledge and wisdom for the AI age and beyond.

**한국어:**
WIA-Wisdom은 인류가 축적한 모든 전통, 철학, 과학, 문화의 지혜를 체계적으로 정리하는 포괄적이고 개방적인 프레임워크입니다. 천년 동안 불교의 지혜를 보존한 팔만대장경처럼, WIA-Wisdom은 AI 시대와 그 이후를 위한 살아있고 진화하는 인류 지식과 지혜의 저장소를 만들고자 합니다.

## Selection Criteria | 선정 기준

Each tradition included in WIA-Wisdom meets these objective criteria:

각 전통은 다음의 객관적 기준을 충족합니다:

1. **Historical Significance | 역사적 중요성**
   - Influenced millions of people over centuries
   - 수세기에 걸쳐 수백만 명에게 영향을 미침

2. **Documented Corpus | 문헌 기반**
   - Has written texts, oral traditions, or systematic practices
   - 문헌, 구전 전통, 또는 체계적 수행법 보유

3. **Wisdom Content | 지혜 내용**
   - Contains ethical, philosophical, or practical wisdom
   - 윤리적, 철학적, 또는 실용적 지혜 포함

4. **Contemporary Relevance | 현대적 관련성**
   - Still practiced, studied, or applicable today
   - 현재도 수행되거나 연구되거나 적용 가능함

5. **Distinct Worldview | 독자적 세계관**
   - Offers unique perspective on human existence
   - 인간 존재에 대한 고유한 관점 제시

## Methodology | 방법론

### Documentation Approach | 문서화 접근법

Each tradition is documented with:

1. **Core Teachings | 핵심 가르침**
   - Fundamental concepts and beliefs
   - Key texts and their interpretations

2. **Practical Wisdom | 실용적 지혜**
   - Daily life applications
   - Ethical guidelines

3. **Contemplative Practices | 명상/수행법**
   - Meditation techniques
   - Rituals and ceremonies

4. **Historical Context | 역사적 맥락**
   - Origins and development
   - Major figures and schools

5. **Modern Integration | 현대적 통합**
   - Contemporary applications
   - Dialogue with other traditions

## Philosophy | 철학

### 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan) | 홍익인간

The guiding principle of WIA-Wisdom is **弘益人間** (Hongik Ingan):

> "Broadly benefit all humanity"
> "널리 인간을 이롭게 하라"

This ancient Korean philosophy, traditionally attributed to the founding of Korea (2333 BCE), aligns perfectly with our mission:

- **Universal Benefit** - Knowledge that serves all, not just some
- **Timeless Wisdom** - Principles that endure across ages
- **Practical Application** - Wisdom for daily life, not just theory
- **Inclusive Approach** - Respecting all traditions without hierarchy
