# Sefirot | 세피로트 (סְפִירוֹת)

> The Ten Divine Emanations: Channels of Creation

---

## Overview | 개요

The Sefirot (Hebrew: סְפִירוֹת, singular: Sefirah) are the ten attributes or emanations through which Ein Sof (the Infinite) reveals itself and continuously creates both the physical realm and the metaphysical chain of higher worlds in Kabbalah. They are often depicted in the symbolic diagram known as the Tree of Life (Etz Chaim), which illustrates their interconnectedness and hierarchy.

The Sefirot should not be understood as ten separate gods, but rather as ten different channels through which the One God reveals His will. Each Sefirah represents many things simultaneously: an aspect of God's nature, a stage in creation, a biblical patriarch or matriarch, and correspondences to the human body and soul.

## Structure: The Three Pillars | 구조: 세 기둥

> "The Tree of Life is the primary mystical symbol of Kabbalah—the structure of the 10 Sefirot, arranged in 3 pillars."

| Pillar | Side | Quality | Sefirot |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| **Pillar of Mercy** | Right | Giving, expansion | Chochmah, Chesed, Netzach |
| **Pillar of Severity** | Left | Restraint, judgment | Binah, Gevurah, Hod |
| **Pillar of Balance** | Middle | Harmony | Keter, Tiferet, Yesod, Malkhut |

### Triadic Groupings:
- **Intellectual**: Keter, Chochmah, Binah (mind)
- **Emotional**: Chesed, Gevurah, Tiferet (heart)
- **Active**: Netzach, Hod, Yesod (action)
- **Receiving**: Malkhut (manifestation)

| 기둥 | 측면 | 품질 | 세피로트 |
|-----|-----|-----|---------|
| **자비의 기둥** | 오른쪽 | 베풂, 확장 | 호크마, 헤세드, 네차흐 |
| **엄격의 기둥** | 왼쪽 | 절제, 심판 | 비나, 게부라, 호드 |

## Da'at: The Hidden Sefirah | 다아트: 숨겨진 세피라

> "Da'at (knowledge) is not actually a Sefirah, but a point of confluence between Wisdom (Chochmah) and Understanding (Binah)."

Da'at represents:
- The integration of wisdom and understanding
- Knowledge that transforms
- The bridge between intellect and emotion
- Sometimes included as an 11th Sefirah

---
## Connection to Ein Sof | 아인 소프와의 연결

> "The sefirot are divine emanations that come from the Ein Sof in a manner often described as a flame."

### The Chain of Emanation:
- Ein Sof (Infinite) is beyond all description
- Emanation flows downward through the Sefirot
- Keter is closest to Ein Sof, least comprehensible
- Malkhut is closest to material world, most accessible
- Energy flows down; consciousness ascends up
