# The Nag Hammadi Library | 나그 함마디 문서

> Ancient Gnostic Treasures Rediscovered

---

## Overview | 개요

The Nag Hammadi library (also known as the Chenoboskion Manuscripts and the Gnostic Gospels) is a collection of early Christian and Gnostic texts discovered near the Upper Egyptian town of Nag Hammadi in 1945. Twelve leather-bound papyrus codices (and a tractate from a thirteenth) buried in a sealed jar were found by an Egyptian farmer named Muhammed al-Samman.

The writings comprise 52 mostly Gnostic treatises, but also three works belonging to the Corpus Hermeticum and a partial translation of Plato's Republic. This discovery revolutionized our understanding of early Christianity and Gnostic traditions.

## The Discovery | 발견

### 1945: Finding the Jar
> "Twelve leather-bound papyrus codices buried in a sealed jar were found by an Egyptian farmer named Muhammed al-Samman and others in late 1945."

### The Location:
- Near Nag Hammadi, Upper Egypt
- Close to ancient Chenoboskion
- Near Pachomian monastery sites

### Current Location:
> "The Nag Hammadi codices are now housed in the Coptic Museum in Cairo, Egypt."

### 1945년: 항아리 발견
> "봉인된 항아리에 묻힌 12개의 가죽으로 묶인 파피루스 코덱스가 1945년 말 무함마드 알-삼만이라는 이집트 농부와 다른 사람들에 의해 발견되었습니다."
### 현재 위치:
> "나그 함마디 코덱스는 현재 이집트 카이로의 콥트 박물관에 소장되어 있습니다."

## Dating and Origins | 연대와 기원

### The Manuscripts:
> "Although the manuscripts discovered at Nag Hammadi are generally dated to the 4th century, there is some debate regarding the original composition of the texts."

> "The codices are written in Coptic, although the individual works are probably all translations from Greek. Scholars generally propose a second-century composition date for most of the lost Greek originals."

### Why Buried?
> "James Robinson suggests that these codices may have belonged to a nearby Pachomian monastery and were buried after Saint Athanasius condemned the use of non-canonical books in his Festal Letter of 367 A.D."

### 사본:
> "나그 함마디에서 발견된 사본은 일반적으로 4세기로 연대가 매겨지지만, 원본 텍스트의 저작에 관해서는 약간의 논쟁이 있습니다."
> "코덱스는 콥트어로 쓰여 있지만, 개별 작품은 아마도 모두 그리스어에서 번역된 것입니다."
### 왜 묻혔나?
> "제임스 로빈슨은 이 코덱스가 근처 파코미우스 수도원에 속했고 367년 성 아타나시우스가 축제 서한에서 비정경 서적 사용을 정죄한 후 묻혔을 수 있다고 제안합니다."

## The Gospel of Thomas | 도마 복음서

> "The best-known of these works is probably the Gospel of Thomas, of which the Nag Hammadi codices contain the only complete text."

### Unique Character:
> "The Gospel of Thomas is very different in tone and structure from other New Testament apocrypha and the four canonical Gospels. Unlike the canonical Gospels, it is not a narrative account of Jesus' life; instead, it consists of logia (sayings) attributed to Jesus."

### Dating:
> "The manuscript of the Coptic text (CG II), found in 1945 at Nag Hammadi in Egypt, is dated at around 340 AD."

> "After the Coptic version of the complete text was discovered in 1945, scholars soon realized that three different Greek text fragments previously found at Oxyrhynchus were part of the Gospel of Thomas. These three papyrus fragments date to between 130 and 250 AD."

> "이 작품 중 가장 잘 알려진 것은 아마도 도마 복음서일 것이며, 나그 함마디 코덱스는 유일한 완전한 텍스트를 포함합니다."
### 독특한 특성:
> "도마 복음서는 다른 신약 외경이나 네 정경 복음서와 어조와 구조에서 매우 다릅니다. 정경 복음서와 달리, 예수의 삶에 대한 서사적 기술이 아닙니다; 대신, 예수에게 귀속된 로기아(말씀)로 구성됩니다."

## Recent Scholarship (2025) | 최근 학문 (2025)

> "One of the big things to happen in the last 10-15 years is that scholars are taking another look at where these books came from, who must have produced them, copied them, and buried them."

> "While these texts are full of gnostic teachings from the second and third centuries, the Nag Hammadi manuscripts were buried in the fourth century. They're products of the fourth century and are written in Coptic."

### Access:
> "It's all online for free at gnosis.org. You can read all of the Nag Hammadi texts translated—good translations for free there."

> "지난 10-15년 동안 일어난 큰 일 중 하나는 학자들이 이 책들이 어디서 왔는지, 누가 생산하고, 복사하고, 묻었을지 다시 살펴보고 있다는 것입니다."
