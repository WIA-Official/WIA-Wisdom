# 06-MYSTICAL | 신비주의 전통

> The esoteric and mystical dimensions of human wisdom

---

## Overview | 개요

Mystical traditions represent the inner, esoteric dimensions found within many religions and philosophies. They emphasize direct experience of the divine, transcendence of ordinary consciousness, and transformation of the self. Though often emerging from specific religious contexts, they share remarkable similarities in their descriptions of ultimate reality.

## Why This Matters | 왜 중요한가
Mystical traditions offer:
- Maps of consciousness and spiritual development
- Practices for direct experience
- Cross-cultural validation (mystics describe similar realities)
- Integration of religion with personal experience
- Antidote to dogmatism and literalism
