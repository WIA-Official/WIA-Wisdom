# 07-MODERN-PHIL | 근현대 철학

> Philosophy from the Enlightenment to the present

---

## Overview | 개요

Modern philosophy, beginning with Descartes and spanning to contemporary thought, represents humanity's attempt to understand existence through reason, experience, and critical analysis. From Enlightenment rationalism to existentialist searches for meaning, this pillar captures the evolution of philosophical inquiry in the modern era.

## Why This Matters | 왜 중요한가
Modern philosophy gave us:
- Framework for secular ethics
- Understanding of consciousness and subjectivity
- Critical tools for analyzing power and society
- Methods for finding meaning without religion
- Foundation for human rights and democracy
