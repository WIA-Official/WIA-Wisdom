# William James | 윌리엄 제임스

> The Father of American Psychology and Pragmatism
> 미국 심리학과 실용주의의 아버지

---

## Overview | 개요

**English:**
William James (1842-1910) was an American philosopher, psychologist, and one of the founders of pragmatism. Along with Charles Sanders Peirce, James established the philosophical school known as pragmatism. Among his most influential books are *The Principles of Psychology*, *Essays in Radical Empiricism*, and *The Varieties of Religious Experience*.

James held that the value of any truth was utterly dependent upon its use to the person who held it. His work bridged philosophy and psychology, science and religion, creating a distinctly American contribution to world philosophy.

**한국어:**
윌리엄 제임스(1842-1910)는 미국의 철학자, 심리학자이자 실용주의의 창시자 중 한 명입니다. 찰스 샌더스 퍼스와 함께 제임스는 실용주의로 알려진 철학 학파를 수립했습니다. 가장 영향력 있는 저서로는 *심리학의 원리*, *급진적 경험론 에세이*, *종교적 경험의 다양성*이 있습니다.

제임스는 어떤 진리의 가치도 그것을 가진 사람에게의 유용성에 전적으로 달려있다고 주장했습니다.

## Pragmatism | 실용주의

**English:**

> "Already in 1898, in a lecture at the University of California on philosophical conceptions and practical results, James had formulated the theory of method known as pragmatism."

### The Pragmatic Method:
> "Originating in the strict analysis of the logic of the sciences made by Charles Sanders Peirce, the theory underwent in James's hands a transforming generalization. He showed how the meaning of any idea—scientific, religious, philosophical, political—can be found ultimately in the succession of experiential consequences that it leads through and to."

### Truth as Usefulness:
James held that:
- Truth is what works in practice
- Ideas become true insofar as they help us function
- Meaning derives from practical consequences

**한국어:**

> "이미 1898년 캘리포니아 대학교에서 철학적 개념과 실제 결과에 관한 강연에서 제임스는 실용주의로 알려진 방법 이론을 공식화했습니다."

### 실용주의적 방법:
> "찰스 샌더스 퍼스가 수행한 과학 논리의 엄격한 분석에서 기원하여, 이 이론은 제임스의 손에서 변형적 일반화를 겪었습니다."

## Radical Empiricism | 급진적 경험론

**English:**

> "In a burst of writing in 1904–5 (collected in *Essays in Radical Empiricism* (1912)) he set out the metaphysical view most commonly known as 'neutral monism,' according to which there is one fundamental 'stuff' that is neither material nor mental."

### Core Principles:
> "William James's view on radical empiricism was rooted in the belief that experience is the foundation of understanding reality. He argued that everything we can know and study must come from direct experiences or connections between experiences."

> "According to James, reality is not divided into separate categories like mind and matter but is instead a continuous flow of experiences and relationships."

### The Three Parts:
> "Radical empiricism consists of a postulate, a statement of fact, and a conclusion. The postulate is that 'the only things that shall be debatable among philosophers shall be things definable in terms drawn from experience.'"

**한국어:**

> "1904-5년의 폭발적인 집필 활동에서(*급진적 경험론 에세이*에 수록) 그는 '중립적 일원론'으로 가장 흔히 알려진 형이상학적 견해를 제시했습니다."

### 핵심 원리:
> "제임스의 급진적 경험론에 대한 견해는 경험이 현실을 이해하는 기초라는 믿음에 뿌리를 두었습니다."

> "제임스에 따르면, 현실은 마음과 물질 같은 분리된 범주로 나뉘지 않고 대신 경험과 관계의 연속적인 흐름입니다."

## Religious Experience | 종교적 경험

**English:**

> "According to James, pragmatism mediates the so-called conflict between science and religion. James took religious experiences very seriously both from a psychologist's perspective and as evidence for the reality of the divine."

### The Will to Believe:
> "Because we do not naturally experience the supernatural, James, the radical empiricist, thinks of faith in God as falling short of knowledge. Yet such faith is pragmatically meaningful to many people."

> "For James, both logic and science have limits beyond which we can legitimately seek the sentiment of rationality. His notorious 'Will to Believe' essay is designed to be a defense of religious faith in the absence of conclusive logical argumentation or scientific evidence."

### Personal and Transformative:
> "William James considered religious experiences deeply personal and transformative, emphasizing their value regardless of whether they could be explained scientifically."

**한국어:**

> "제임스에 따르면, 실용주의는 이른바 과학과 종교 사이의 갈등을 중재합니다."

### 믿으려는 의지:
> "우리가 자연적으로 초자연적인 것을 경험하지 않기 때문에, 급진적 경험론자인 제임스는 하나님에 대한 믿음이 지식에 미치지 못한다고 생각합니다. 그러나 그러한 믿음은 많은 사람들에게 실용적으로 의미있습니다."

> "윌리엄 제임스는 종교적 경험을 깊이 개인적이고 변형적인 것으로 여겼으며, 과학적으로 설명될 수 있는지 여부와 관계없이 그 가치를 강조했습니다."
