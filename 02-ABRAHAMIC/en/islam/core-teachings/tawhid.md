# Tawhid | 타우히드 (توحيد)

> The Oneness of God: Islam's Central Doctrine

---

## Overview | 개요

Tawhid, literally meaning "to unite" or "to make one," refers to the principle of monotheism in Islam. It is the religion's central and single most important concept, upon which a Muslim's entire religious adherence rests. It unequivocally holds that God is indivisibly one (ahad) and single (wahid).

Tawhid constitutes the foremost article of the Muslim profession of faith. According to Islamic theology, it is the foundational principle that determines how you understand literally everything—worship, morality, science, relationships, purpose, and reality itself.

---
## The Shahada | 샤하다 (信仰告白)

> "Tawhid constitutes the foremost article of the Muslim profession of submission. The first part of the Islamic declaration of faith (shahada) is the declaration of belief in the oneness of God."

### The Declaration:
**Arabic:** لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ

**Translation:** "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger."

> "The Shahada is a verbal expression of Tawheed, affirming belief in Allah's Oneness and Muhammad's (PBUH) prophethood."

### 선언:
> "샤하다는 알라의 유일성과 무함마드(평화가 그에게)의 예언자성에 대한 믿음을 확언하는 타우히드의 언어적 표현입니다."
---
## Three Categories of Tawhid | 타우히드의 세 범주

> "Islamic scholars have categorized Tawhid into three interconnected types."

### 1. Tawhid al-Rububiyyah (Lordship)
> "Tawhid al-Rububiyyah refers to the oneness of God in terms of His lordship over the universe. It emphasizes that Allah is the sole creator, sustainer, and controller of everything."

### 2. Tawhid al-Uluhiyyah (Worship)
> "Tawhid al-Uluhiyyah asserts the oneness of Allah in worship. Muslims believe that all acts of worship, supplication, and devotion must be directed solely to Allah, rejecting any form of association or partnership in worship."

### 3. Tawhid al-Asma wa Sifat (Names and Attributes)
> "Tawhid al-Asma wa Sifat refers to the uniqueness of Allah's names and attributes. This aspect acknowledges that Allah possesses qualities that are perfect and unmatched."

### 1. 타우히드 알-루부비야 (주권)
> "타우히드 알-루부비야는 우주에 대한 주권의 측면에서 하나님의 유일성을 말합니다. 알라가 모든 것의 유일한 창조자, 유지자, 통제자임을 강조합니다."
### 2. 타우히드 알-울루히야 (예배)
> "타우히드 알-울루히야는 예배에서 알라의 유일성을 주장합니다. 모든 예배, 간구, 헌신의 행위는 오직 알라에게만 향해야 합니다."
### 3. 타우히드 알-아스마 와 시파트 (이름과 속성)
> "타우히드 알-아스마 와 시파트는 알라의 이름과 속성의 고유함을 말합니다."

## Shirk: The Opposite of Tawhid | 시르크: 타우히드의 반대

> "To attribute divinity to anything or anyone else is considered shirk, which is an unpardonable sin unless repented afterwards, according to the Qur'an."

### Types of Shirk:
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Major (Shirk Akbar)** | Associating partners with Allah in worship |
| **Minor (Shirk Asghar)** | Hidden polytheism (showing off in worship) |
| **Hidden (Shirk Khafi)** | Subtle forms of divided loyalty |

### The Gravity:
Shirk is the one sin that Allah will not forgive if one dies without repenting of it (Quran 4:48).

> "다른 무엇이나 누군가에게 신성을 귀속시키는 것은 시르크로 간주되며, 꾸란에 따르면 나중에 회개하지 않으면 용서받을 수 없는 죄입니다."
| 유형 | 설명 |
|-----|-----|
| **대 시르크** | 예배에서 알라와 동반자를 연관 |
| **소 시르크** | 숨겨진 다신론 (예배에서 과시) |
| **은밀한 시르크** | 미묘한 형태의 분열된 충성 |

## Scholarly Approaches | 학문적 접근

> "Understanding of the meaning of 'tawhid' is one of the most controversial issues for Muslims. Islamic scholars have different approaches toward understanding it, comprising textualistic approach, theological approach, philosophical approach, and the Sufi and Irfani approach."

### Theological Schools:
| School | Emphasis |
|--------|----------|
| **Ash'ari** | Rational theology with traditional limits |
| **Maturidi** | Reason and revelation in balance |
| **Athari** | Literal textual approach |
| **Sufi/Irfani** | Experiential unity with God |

> "Throughout Islamic history, various theological schools have emerged, each with its approach to understanding Allah's names and attributes."

| 학파 | 강조점 |
|-----|-------|
| **아샤리** | 전통적 한계 내의 이성적 신학 |
| **마투리디** | 이성과 계시의 균형 |
| **아타리** | 문자적 텍스트 접근 |
| **수피/이르파니** | 하나님과의 경험적 합일 |
