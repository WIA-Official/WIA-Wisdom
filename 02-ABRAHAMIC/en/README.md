# 02-ABRAHAMIC | 아브라함계 신앙

> The three great monotheistic traditions tracing to Abraham

---

## Overview | 개요

Judaism, Christianity, and Islam share a common ancestor in the patriarch Abraham and emphasize ethical monotheism, prophetic revelation, and covenantal relationship with the Divine. Together, they encompass nearly 4 billion adherents and have profoundly shaped human history, ethics, law, and culture.

## Why This Matters | 왜 중요한가
Abrahamic traditions have shaped:
- Western law and ethics (Ten Commandments, natural law)
- Human rights concepts (dignity, equality)
- Social justice movements
- Art, architecture, literature across civilizations
- Dialogue between faith and reason
