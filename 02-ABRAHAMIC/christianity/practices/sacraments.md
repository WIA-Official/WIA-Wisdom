# The Sacraments | 성사/성례 (聖事/聖禮)

> Sacred Rites: Visible Signs of Invisible Grace
> 성스러운 예식: 보이지 않는 은혜의 보이는 표징

---

## Overview | 개요

**English:**
The sacraments are sacred rites in Christianity seen as visible signs and efficacious channels of God's grace to all who receive them with proper disposition. The word "sacrament" comes from the Latin *sacramentum*, meaning a sacred oath or mystery.

In the 12th century, Peter Lombard defined seven sacraments for the Western church: baptism, confirmation, Eucharist, penance, holy orders, matrimony, and extreme unction. These were affirmed by the Council of Trent (1545-63). Classical Protestant churches (Lutheran, Anglican, Reformed) recognize only two—baptism and Eucharist—as instituted by Christ in the Gospels.

**한국어:**
성사(성례)는 기독교에서 적절한 자세로 받는 모든 이에게 하나님 은혜의 보이는 표징이자 효과적인 통로로 여겨지는 성스러운 예식입니다. "성사"라는 단어는 라틴어 *사크라멘툼*에서 왔으며 성스러운 서약이나 신비를 의미합니다.

12세기에 베드로 롬바르두스가 서방 교회를 위해 일곱 성사를 정의했습니다: 세례, 견진, 성체, 고해, 신품, 혼인, 병자성사. 이것들은 트리엔트 공의회(1545-63)에서 확인되었습니다. 고전 개신교 교회(루터교, 성공회, 개혁교회)는 복음서에서 그리스도가 제정한 세례와 성찬 두 가지만 인정합니다.

## The Seven Sacraments | 일곱 성사

**English:**

### 1. Baptism (세례):
> "By Baptism we become members of the Church, the Body of Christ."

- Entry into the Christian community
- Cleansing from original sin
- Rebirth in the Holy Spirit
- Performed only once in a person's life

### 2. Confirmation (견진):
- Strengthening of baptismal grace
- Receiving the fullness of the Holy Spirit
- Commitment to mature faith
- Anointing with sacred oil (chrism)

### 3. Eucharist (성체/성찬):
> "The source and summit of Christian living"

- Sharing in Christ's body and blood
- Memorial of Christ's sacrifice
- Thanksgiving (Greek: *eucharistia*)
- Central act of Christian worship

### 4. Penance/Reconciliation (고해/화해):
- Confession of sins
- Absolution by priest
- Reconciliation with God and Church
- Spiritual healing

### 5. Anointing of the Sick (병자성사):
- For the seriously ill or dying
- Spiritual and sometimes physical healing
- Preparation for death
- Forgiveness of sins

### 6. Holy Orders (신품):
- Ordination of deacons, priests, bishops
- Service to the Church
- Authority to celebrate sacraments
- Apostolic succession

### 7. Matrimony (혼인):
- Sacred bond between spouses
- Reflection of Christ's union with Church
- Covenant relationship
- Grace for family life

**한국어:**

### 1. 세례:
- 그리스도인 공동체에 진입
- 원죄로부터의 정화
- 성령 안에서 거듭남

### 2. 견진:
- 세례 은총의 강화
- 성령의 충만함을 받음

### 3. 성체/성찬:
- 그리스도의 몸과 피에 참여
- 그리스도 희생의 기념

### 4. 고해/화해:
- 죄의 고백
- 사제에 의한 사죄
- 하나님과 교회와의 화해

### 5. 병자성사:
- 중병자나 임종자를 위함
- 영적, 때로는 육체적 치유

### 6. 신품:
- 부제, 사제, 주교의 서품

### 7. 혼인:
- 배우자 간의 성스러운 유대
- 그리스도와 교회의 결합 반영

## Theological Understanding | 신학적 이해

**English:**

### Catholic View:
> "The seven sacraments are ceremonies that point to what is sacred, significant and important for Christians. They are special occasions for experiencing God's saving presence."

- Sacraments confer grace ex opere operato (by the work performed)
- Valid when proper form, matter, and intention are present
- Instituted by Christ

### Protestant Views:
| Tradition | Sacraments | Understanding |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Lutheran** | Baptism, Eucharist, (Penance) | Means of grace |
| **Reformed** | Baptism, Eucharist | Signs and seals of covenant |
| **Anglican** | Baptism, Eucharist + 5 others | Outward signs of inward grace |
| **Baptist** | Baptism, Lord's Supper | Ordinances, not sacraments |

**한국어:**

### 가톨릭 견해:
- 성사는 행해지는 행위에 의해 은총을 전달함
- 적절한 형식, 질료, 의도가 있을 때 유효함
- 그리스도에 의해 제정됨

## Baptism and Eucharist | 세례와 성찬

**English:**

### Baptism:
> "Immersion in water symbolizes not only death and purification, but also regeneration and renewal."

| Element | Symbolism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Water** | Cleansing, death, rebirth |
| **Trinitarian Formula** | Father, Son, Holy Spirit |
| **White Garment** | New life in Christ |
| **Candle** | Light of Christ |

### Eucharist:
> "During the mass the priest consecrates bread and wine, which are transubstantiated into the body and blood of Christ."

| Element | Catholic View | Protestant View |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Bread** | Becomes Christ's body | Memorial/spiritual presence |
| **Wine** | Becomes Christ's blood | Memorial/spiritual presence |

**한국어:**

### 세례:
| 요소 | 상징 |
|-----|------|
| **물** | 정화, 죽음, 거듭남 |
| **삼위일체 공식** | 성부, 성자, 성령 |
| **흰 예복** | 그리스도 안에서의 새 생명 |

## Denominational Differences | 교파별 차이

**English:**

| Tradition | Number | View |
|-----------|--------|------|
| **Roman Catholic** | 7 | Channels of grace instituted by Christ |
| **Eastern Orthodox** | 7 (Mysteries) | Divine mysteries for salvation |
| **Lutheran** | 2-3 | Gospel promises with visible elements |
| **Reformed/Presbyterian** | 2 | Signs and seals of covenant grace |
| **Methodist** | 2 | Means of grace |
| **Baptist** | 2 (Ordinances) | Symbolic acts of obedience |
| **Quaker** | 0 | Inward experience over outward forms |
