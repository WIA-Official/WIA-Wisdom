# CORE - Universal Principles | 핵심 원칙

> The heart of WIA-Wisdom: principles that transcend all traditions
> WIA-Wisdom의 핵심: 모든 전통을 초월하는 원칙

---

## Purpose | 목적

**English:**
CORE contains the universal principles, ethical foundations, and synthesis methodology that runs through all 9 Pillars. This is not a "meta-religion" but a recognition that across all human wisdom traditions, certain themes and insights recur.

**한국어:**
CORE는 9개의 기둥을 관통하는 보편 원칙, 윤리적 기반, 그리고 종합 방법론을 담고 있습니다. 이것은 "상위 종교"가 아니라, 인류의 모든 지혜 전통에서 특정 주제와 통찰이 반복적으로 나타난다는 것을 인정하는 것입니다.

## The Guiding Principle | 지도 원칙

### 弘益人間 (Hongik Ingan)

> "Broadly benefit all humanity"
> "널리 인간을 이롭게 하라"

This ancient Korean principle (traditionally dated to 2333 BCE) expresses a universal aspiration found across traditions:

| Tradition | Expression |
|-----------|------------|
| Sanskrit | लोकसंग्रह (Lokasaṅgraha) - welfare of all |
| Hebrew | תיקון עולם (Tikkun Olam) - repair the world |
| Greek | κοινωνία (Koinonia) - community/fellowship |
| Chinese | 大同 (Dàtóng) - Great Unity |
| Ubuntu | "I am because we are" |

## Core Documents | 핵심 문서

### principles.md - Universal Principles | 보편 원칙
Recurring themes across all traditions:
- The Golden Rule variations
- Interdependence/interconnection
- Inner cultivation
- Compassion/love
- Justice/righteousness
- Wisdom/knowledge seeking

### ethics.md - Cross-Tradition Ethics | 범전통 윤리
Ethical foundations found universally:
- Respect for life
- Truth and honesty
- Care for the vulnerable
- Stewardship of nature
- Balance and moderation

### synthesis.md - Integration Methodology | 통합 방법론
How we bring traditions together:
- Comparative approach (similarities)
- Contrastive approach (differences)
- Dialogical approach (conversation)
- Practical approach (application)

## What CORE Is NOT | CORE가 아닌 것

- ❌ A new religion
- ❌ Ranking of traditions
- ❌ Syncretism (mixing into one)
- ❌ Reductionism (simplifying differences)
- ❌ Supremacism (claiming one is best)

## What CORE IS | CORE인 것

- ✅ A framework for understanding
- ✅ Recognition of common humanity
- ✅ Respect for diversity
- ✅ Tool for dialogue
- ✅ Resource for wisdom seekers
