# The Medicine Wheel | 메디신 휠

> Sacred Circle of Life and Wisdom
> 생명과 지혜의 신성한 원

---

## Overview | 개요

**English:**
The Medicine Wheel, also known as the Sacred Hoop, is a fundamental symbol and practice deeply rooted in the spiritual traditions of many Indigenous peoples of North America. This ancient emblem represents the interconnectedness of all things, the cycles of life, and the journey towards wholeness and balance.

The Medicine Wheel has been used by generations of various Native American tribes for health and healing. It embodies the Four Directions, as well as Father Sky, Mother Earth, and Spirit Tree—all symbolizing dimensions of health and the cycles of life.

**한국어:**
메디신 휠은 세이크리드 훕으로도 알려져 있으며, 북미 많은 원주민 민족의 영적 전통에 깊이 뿌리박힌 근본적인 상징이자 실천입니다. 이 고대 상징은 모든 것의 상호연결성, 생명의 순환, 완전함과 균형을 향한 여정을 나타냅니다.

메디신 휠은 다양한 아메리카 원주민 부족의 여러 세대에 걸쳐 건강과 치유에 사용되어 왔습니다.

---

## The Structure | 구조

**English:**

> "In its most simplistic definition, the Medicine Wheel is a symbol of ALL creation, of all races of people, birds, fish, animals, trees, and stones. Its shape is that of a wagon wheel made of stones."

> "According to tribal beliefs, the circular shape of the wheel represents the earth, the sun, the moon, the cycles of life, the seasons, and day to night."

### Four Quadrants:
> "It's comprised of four quadrants, which represent several concepts: seasons of the year, stages of life, cardinal directions, and the four sides of a person—physical, emotional, spiritual, mental."

**한국어:**

> "가장 단순한 정의에서 메디신 휠은 모든 창조물, 모든 인종, 새, 물고기, 동물, 나무, 돌의 상징입니다."

### 네 사분면:
> "네 사분면으로 구성되어 있으며, 여러 개념을 나타냅니다: 일년의 계절, 삶의 단계, 기본 방위, 그리고 사람의 네 면—신체적, 감정적, 영적, 정신적."

## The Four Directions | 네 방향

**English:**

> "The Native American tradition of recognizing the Four Directions—East, South, West, and North—and the central concept of the Medicine Wheel capture profound spiritual significance and cultural values across numerous indigenous communities."

| Direction | Color | Season | Life Stage | Element |
|-----------|-------|--------|------------|---------|
| **East** | Yellow/Red | Spring | Birth/Childhood | Air/Fire |
| **South** | Red/Yellow | Summer | Youth | Earth/Water |
| **West** | Black | Autumn | Adulthood | Water |
| **North** | White | Winter | Elderhood | Earth/Air |

> "Each of the cardinal directions holds a unique symbolic meaning across many Native American traditions. The directions are commonly associated with specific colors, elements, animals, life stages, seasons, sacred plants, and spiritual attributes."

**한국어:**

| 방향 | 색깔 | 계절 | 생애 단계 | 요소 |
|-----|-----|-----|---------|-----|
| **동** | 노랑/빨강 | 봄 | 탄생/유년기 | 공기/불 |
| **남** | 빨강/노랑 | 여름 | 청년기 | 땅/물 |
| **서** | 검정 | 가을 | 성년기 | 물 |
| **북** | 하양 | 겨울 | 노년기 | 땅/공기 |

## The Number Four | 숫자 4

**English:**

> "The number four is sacred to many Native American tribes as it represents the four seasons, the four human needs—physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual—the four kingdoms—animal, mineral, plant, and human—and the four sacred medicines—sweetgrass, tobacco, cedar, and sage."

### Four Sacred Medicines:
| Medicine | Use |
|----------|-----|
| **Sweetgrass** | Attraction of positive energies |
| **Tobacco** | Offerings and prayers |
| **Cedar** | Purification and protection |
| **Sage** | Cleansing and healing |

**한국어:**

> "숫자 4는 많은 아메리카 원주민 부족에게 신성합니다. 네 계절, 네 가지 인간 필요—신체적, 정신적, 감정적, 영적—네 왕국—동물, 광물, 식물, 인간—그리고 네 가지 신성한 약초—스위트그래스, 담배, 삼나무, 세이지—를 나타내기 때문입니다."

## Seven Directions | 일곱 방향

**English:**

> "Although the medicine wheel may seem to consist only of four parts, there are actually seven very essential directions to know."

| Direction | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **East** | Sunrise, new beginnings |
| **South** | Growth, warmth |
| **West** | Sunset, introspection |
| **North** | Wisdom, ancestors |
| **Above** | Father Sky, spiritual realm |
| **Below** | Mother Earth, grounding |
| **Center** | You—your physical and spiritual state |

> "The last direction, often forgotten, is you—this refers to your physical and spiritual state at the center of the wheel."

**한국어:**

| 방향 | 의미 |
|-----|-----|
| **동** | 일출, 새 시작 |
| **남** | 성장, 따스함 |
| **서** | 일몰, 내성 |
| **북** | 지혜, 조상 |
| **위** | 하늘 아버지, 영적 영역 |
| **아래** | 대지 어머니, 뿌리내림 |
| **중심** | 당신—당신의 신체적, 영적 상태 |

## Tribal Variations | 부족별 변형

**English:**

> "Different tribes and nations, including the Lakota, Cheyenne, Crow, and others, have their own interpretations and traditions associated with the Medicine Wheel."

> "Many people have different interpretations in their medicine wheel and they are all right. No one is wrong. In the centre of the circle is the Creator's name, because without the Creator nothing would exist."

**한국어:**

> "라코타, 샤이엔, 크로 등 다양한 부족과 민족이 메디신 휠과 관련된 자체 해석과 전통을 가지고 있습니다."

> "많은 사람들이 자신의 메디신 휠에서 다른 해석을 가지고 있으며 모두 맞습니다. 누구도 틀리지 않습니다."

## Cultural Respect | 문화적 존중

**English:**

> "It's crucial to approach the Medicine Wheel with respect and cultural sensitivity. The Medicine Wheel is a sacred symbol and practice, and it is essential to learn from and respect the Indigenous cultures from which it originates."

**한국어:**

> "메디신 휠에 존중과 문화적 감수성으로 접근하는 것이 중요합니다. 메디신 휠은 신성한 상징이자 실천이며, 그것이 기원한 원주민 문화를 배우고 존중하는 것이 필수적입니다."
