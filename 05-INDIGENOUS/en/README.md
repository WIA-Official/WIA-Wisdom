# 05-INDIGENOUS | 토착 지혜

> Wisdom traditions of native peoples worldwide

---

## Overview | 개요

Indigenous wisdom represents humanity's oldest and most geographically diverse knowledge systems. From African traditions to Native American spirituality, Australian Aboriginal Dreamtime to Siberian shamanism, these traditions offer profound insights into human relationship with nature, community, ancestors, and the sacred.

## Why This Matters | 왜 중요한가
Indigenous wisdom offers:
- Ecological knowledge (thousands of years of sustainability)
- Community-based values (Ubuntu: "I am because we are")
- Healing practices integrated with worldview
- Alternative to individualistic materialism
- Respect for non-human life and future generations
