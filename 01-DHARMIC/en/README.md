# 01-DHARMIC | 인도계 전통

> Wisdom traditions originating from the Indian subcontinent

---

## Overview | 개요

The Dharmic traditions share a common philosophical vocabulary (dharma, karma, moksha, samsara) while offering distinct paths to understanding reality and liberation. Despite their differences, they represent some of humanity's most sophisticated explorations of consciousness, ethics, and ultimate reality.

## Shared Concepts | 공통 개념
| Concept | Sanskrit | Meaning | 의미 |
|---------|----------|---------|------|
| Dharma | धर्म | Cosmic order, duty, truth | 우주 질서, 의무, 진리 |
| Karma | कर्म | Action and its consequences | 행위와 그 결과 |
| Samsara | संसार | Cycle of rebirth | 윤회 |
| Moksha/Nirvana | मोक्ष/निर्वाण | Liberation | 해탈 |
| Ahimsa | अहिंसा | Non-violence | 비폭력 |

## Why This Matters | 왜 중요한가
Dharmic traditions offer some of humanity's deepest explorations of:
- The nature of consciousness and self
- Psychological understanding (millennia before modern psychology)
- Ethical frameworks based on karma and interconnection
- Contemplative practices verified through personal experience
