# Milarepa (མི་ལ་རས་པ) | 밀라레파

> From Black Magician to Enlightened Yogi: Tibet's Greatest Poet-Saint

---

## Overview | 개요

Milarepa (c. 1028/40–1111/23) was a Tibetan Buddhist yogi, poet, and one of the most beloved figures in Tibetan Buddhism. His life story—from a murderer who used black magic to avenge his family, to an enlightened master who achieved Buddhahood in a single lifetime—has inspired practitioners for nearly a millennium.

> Milarepa is famous for his songs (*dohas*) and poems, which express the essence of Buddhist teachings in vivid, accessible language. His biography demonstrates that enlightenment is possible for anyone, regardless of past sins, through genuine devotion and perseverance.

He is considered one of the founders of the Kagyu lineage and the most important disciple of Marpa the Translator.

> 밀라레파는 불교 가르침의 본질을 생생하고 접근하기 쉬운 언어로 표현한 그의 노래 (*도하*)와 시로 유명합니다. 그의 전기는 과거의 죄에 관계없이 진정한 헌신과 인내를 통해 누구에게나 깨달음이 가능하다는 것을 보여줍니다.

## Life Story | 생애 이야기
> Milarepa's life is traditionally divided into three parts: his early years of suffering and revenge, his years of hardship under Marpa, and his years of solitary meditation and teaching.

### Youth and Revenge
> After his father's death, Milarepa's family was cheated of their inheritance by his uncle and aunt. His mother, consumed by bitterness, sent young Milarepa to learn black magic. He killed 35 people at a wedding celebration using sorcery.

### Seeking the Dharma
> Overwhelmed by remorse, Milarepa sought out Marpa the Translator. Marpa subjected him to years of brutal hardships—building and tearing down towers repeatedly—to purify his karma before giving him the teachings.

### Enlightenment
> After receiving the complete teachings, Milarepa spent years in isolated caves, wearing only cotton cloth (*repa*) and eating only nettles, until he achieved complete enlightenment.

### 청년기와 복수
> 아버지의 사망 후, 밀라레파의 가족은 숙부와 숙모에게 유산을 빼앗겼습니다. 쓴맛에 사로잡힌 그의 어머니는 젊은 밀라레파를 흑마법을 배우도록 보냈습니다. 그는 주술을 사용하여 결혼 축하에서 35명을 죽였습니다.
### 법을 찾아서
> 회한에 압도되어, 밀라레파는 번역가 마르파를 찾았습니다. 마르파는 가르침을 주기 전 그의 업을 정화하기 위해 탑을 반복적으로 짓고 허무는 수년간의 혹독한 고행을 그에게 주었습니다.
### 깨달음
> 완전한 가르침을 받은 후, 밀라레파는 오직 면천 (*레파*)만 입고 쐐기풀만 먹으며 외딴 동굴에서 수년을 보내다가 완전한 깨달음을 성취했습니다.

## The Songs of Milarepa | 밀라레파의 노래
> Milarepa communicated the Dharma primarily through spontaneous songs. His *Hundred Thousand Songs* remains one of the great treasures of Tibetan literature.

Sample verse:
> "This body of flesh, the result of karma,
> The mind, confused by ignorance,

Themes in his songs:
- **Impermanence**: The urgency of practice
- **Emptiness**: The nature of mind
- **Devotion**: Gratitude to the guru
- **Renunciation**: Freedom from attachment

> 밀라레파는 주로 즉흥적인 노래를 통해 법을 전했습니다. 그의 『십만가』는 티베트 문학의 위대한 보물 중 하나로 남아 있습니다.
> "업의 결과인 이 살의 몸은
> 무지에 의해 혼란스러운 마음은

## Teachings and Practice | 가르침과 수행
> Milarepa's approach emphasized direct experience over scholarly learning. He practiced the Six Yogas of Naropa and Mahamudra meditation in extreme conditions.

Key teachings:
- **Tummo** (inner heat): Generating warmth through meditation
- **Mahamudra**: Direct recognition of mind's nature
- **Guru devotion**: Complete surrender to the teacher
- **Solitary retreat**: Intensive isolated practice

> "When you run after your thoughts, you are like a dog chasing a stick... but if you let thoughts come and go without attachment, they become like clouds passing through the sky."

> 밀라레파의 접근은 학문적 배움보다 직접 경험을 강조했습니다. 그는 극한 조건에서 나로파의 여섯 요가와 마하무드라 명상을 수행했습니다.

## Legacy | 유산
> Milarepa's main disciple, Gampopa, systematized his teachings and established the Kagyu school's monastic tradition. The Kagyu lineage continues to this day, and Milarepa remains its most revered figure.

His story demonstrates:
- Karma can be purified
- Enlightenment is possible in one lifetime
- Genuine devotion overcomes all obstacles
- Simple practice surpasses intellectual knowledge

> 밀라레파의 주요 제자 감포파는 그의 가르침을 체계화하고 까규파의 승려 전통을 확립했습니다. 까규 계보는 오늘날까지 계속되며, 밀라레파는 가장 존경받는 인물로 남아 있습니다.
