# 04-HELLENIC | 헬레니즘 철학

> The philosophical traditions of ancient Greece and Rome

---

## Overview | 개요

Greek philosophy represents the foundation of Western rational inquiry. From the Pre-Socratics' questions about nature to Plato's Forms, Aristotle's logic, and Stoic ethics, Hellenic thought established methods of reasoning, ethical frameworks, and metaphysical systems that continue to shape global discourse.
