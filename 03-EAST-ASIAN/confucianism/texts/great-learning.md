# The Great Learning | 대학 (大學)

> From Self-Cultivation to World Peace
> 수신에서 치천하까지

---

## Overview | 개요

**English:**
The Great Learning (Daxue, 大學) is one of the Four Books (Sishu) of Confucianism, traditionally attributed to Confucius's disciple Zengzi. Originally a chapter within the Book of Rites (Liji), it was elevated to canonical status by Neo-Confucian scholars, particularly Zhu Xi (1130-1200), who made it part of the essential curriculum for Chinese education.

This concise treatise presents a systematic program of moral self-cultivation (xiushen) that extends outward from the individual to family, state, and world. Its famous "eight steps" describe a progressive path from investigating things to bringing peace to all under heaven—a vision of personal development as the foundation for social and political harmony.

**한국어:**
대학(大學)은 유교의 사서(四書) 중 하나로, 전통적으로 공자의 제자 증자에게 귀속됩니다. 원래 예기(禮記) 안의 한 장이었으나, 신유학자들, 특히 주희(1130-1200)에 의해 경전적 지위로 격상되어 중국 교육의 필수 교과과정의 일부가 되었습니다.

이 간결한 논고는 개인에서 가정, 국가, 세계로 확장되는 도덕적 수기(修己)의 체계적 프로그램을 제시합니다. 유명한 "팔조목"은 격물에서 천하평화까지의 점진적 경로를 설명합니다.

## The Eight Steps | 팔조목

**English:**

> "From the Son of Heaven down to the mass of the people, all must consider the cultivation of the person the root of everything besides."

### The Famous Progression:

| Step | Chinese | English | Direction |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | 格物 | Investigate things | Inward |
| 2 | 致知 | Extend knowledge | |
| 3 | 誠意 | Make intentions sincere | |
| 4 | 正心 | Rectify the mind | |
| 5 | 修身 | Cultivate the person | Center |
| 6 | 齊家 | Order the family | |
| 7 | 治國 | Govern the state | |
| 8 | 平天下 | Bring peace to the world | Outward |

### The Classic Statement:
> "Wishing to order well their States, they first regulated their families. Wishing to regulate their families, they first cultivated their persons. Wishing to cultivate their persons, they first rectified their hearts. Wishing to rectify their hearts, they first sought to be sincere in their thoughts. Wishing to be sincere in their thoughts, they first extended to the utmost their knowledge. Such extension of knowledge lay in the investigation of things."

## Self-Cultivation as Root | 수신이 근본

**English:**

> "Self-cultivation 修其身 (xiu qishen) is a perennial concern in Confucianism."

### Why Self-Cultivation First?
- Change must begin with oneself
- Outer harmony reflects inner harmony
- Leaders must exemplify virtue
- Society is transformed person by person

> "Through self-cultivation one can bring order and harmony to one's mind, personal life, family, state and the world as a whole."

**한국어:**

### 왜 수신이 먼저인가?
- 변화는 자신에게서 시작해야 함
- 외적 조화는 내적 조화를 반영
- 지도자는 덕을 본보기로 보여야 함
- 사회는 사람 하나하나에 의해 변화됨

## Historical Significance | 역사적 의의

**English:**

### Neo-Confucian Elevation:
> "The text remained relatively obscure until the Neo-Confucian period when it was thrust into the philosophical limelight."

| Scholar | Period | Contribution |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| **Cheng Hao** | 1032-1085 | First to recognize as separate treatise |
| **Cheng Yi** | 1033-1107 | Emphasized its importance |
| **Zhu Xi** | 1130-1200 | Divided into classic and commentary |

### The Four Books:
The Great Learning, along with the Analects, Mencius, and Doctrine of the Mean, became the foundation of Chinese education and the imperial examination system.

**한국어:**

| 학자 | 시기 | 기여 |
|-----|-----|------|
| **정호** | 1032-1085 | 별도 논고로 처음 인정 |
| **정이** | 1033-1107 | 중요성 강조 |
| **주희** | 1130-1200 | 경(經)과 전(傳)으로 분리 |

## Key Concepts | 핵심 개념

**English:**

### Investigation of Things (Gewu 格物):
Different interpretations exist:
- **Zhu Xi**: Study external things to understand principle
- **Wang Yangming**: Rectify the mind's relation to things

### Sincerity of Intention (Chengyi 誠意):
Making one's intentions genuine, without self-deception.

### Rectifying the Mind (Zhengxin 正心):
Removing emotions that disturb clear judgment.

**한국어:**

### 격물 (格物):
다양한 해석이 존재합니다:
- **주희**: 외부 사물을 연구하여 이치를 이해
- **왕양명**: 사물에 대한 마음의 관계를 바로잡음

### 성의 (誠意):
자기기만 없이 뜻을 진실하게 함

### 정심 (正心):
명확한 판단을 방해하는 감정 제거

## Influence | 영향

**English:**

### Educational Impact:
- Core text for civil service examinations
- Foundation of East Asian education for centuries
- Still studied in contemporary philosophy

### Political Philosophy:
- Model for ethical governance
- Self-cultivation as prerequisite for leadership
- Personal virtue as basis of social order

**한국어:**

### 교육적 영향:
- 과거시험의 핵심 텍스트
- 수 세기 동안 동아시아 교육의 기반
- 현대 철학에서도 여전히 연구됨
